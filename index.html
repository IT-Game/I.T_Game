Run For Your Life Game

Rules!

1. The name of the game is Run For Your Life.
2.Our main objective is to pass the course by winning more than 750 points.
3. You have to continuously press your up arrow to proceed up and to go down release your arrow.
4. Since this is a virtual game, all you need to do is open the link and play.
5. This is a single-player game, that helps in your focus.
6. There will be obstacles thrown at you and you have to use your arrows to control your movement.
7. If you touch the obstacle or touch the end of the screen, you will lose.
8. After you cross 750 points you will automatically win.
9. Good Luck :)
